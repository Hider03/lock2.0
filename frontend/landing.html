About the Project

Lock 2.0 (LAF) is a next-generation platform for organizing and safeguarding personal items. Whether you’re tracking valuable belongings, sharing equipment with a team, or keeping a digital record of your inventory, LAF helps you stay organized and informed in real time.

Key features will include:

  • Add and manage items with detailed information
  • View statistics and usage history at a glance
  • Stay updated with a live activity feed
  • Access your data securely from any device
